Public mailing list and archives

Discussion of the URI syntax and related Internet Drafts takes place on the URI working group mailing list <uri@w3.org>. A hypertext archive of the URI mailing list is available.

Review of new URI scheme proposals and of the process for URI scheme registration takes place on the URI Scheme Review mailing list <uri-review@ietf.org>.
